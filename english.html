Main Solutions



Agribusiness Intelligence System (SMARTAGRO)

   Intelligent agriculture or Smart Agro is a concept that aims to coordinate farm management with data technology to optimize the complex systems and changing dynamics of agriculture. The Smart Agro also aims to make rural activities more efficient, thanks to real-time data access that allows the producer to make more accurate decisions.

Port Intelligence System (SMARTPORT)

   Port intelligence includes the rational use of massive data and real-time monitoring of port operations. In that In this case, port intelligence services are of great help to improve the efficiency of logistical operations, after all, they allow that you closely monitor several factors. The advantage of applying this feature is to provide strategic information with direct impact in real-time operations. Without them, many of the actors in the process would not have idea of ​​what is really happening in the ports they operate.

Technology 5G e IOT

   5G is the fifth generation of technology and communication standard wireless, the network that mobile devices will use to connect to the internet from anywhere. In addition to 5G being able to reach a speed of up to 100 times or more compared to 4G, the new generation also promises low latency, that is, a minimum time response between a device and the servers.
